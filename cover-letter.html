To Whom It May Concern:

I am writing to express my interest in the counseling position with your organization. As a Clinical Mental Health Counseling graduate student at Grand Canyon University, I am passionate about helping individuals navigate life's challenges, build resilience, and achieve meaningful personal growth. My academic training and hands-on clinical experience have prepared me to provide compassionate, ethical, and evidence-based care to diverse populations.

Currently, I am completing my counseling internship at Pinnacle Recovery Center, where I provide individual counseling services under supervision, facilitate psychoeducational and process groups, assist with treatment planning, and maintain clinical documentation. This experience has strengthened my ability to build therapeutic rapport, collaborate within multidisciplinary teams, and support clients as they work toward recovery and long-term wellness. I have gained valuable experience utilizing interventions informed by Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT), Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT), and trauma-informed care.

Prior to my current internship, I completed my practicum at Life Stone Counseling Center, where I assisted with client assessments, observed counseling sessions, participated in case consultation, and developed foundational skills in treatment planning and case conceptualization. These experiences have reinforced my commitment to the counseling profession and strengthened my confidence in supporting clients through a variety of mental health concerns.

What draws me most to counseling is the opportunity to create a safe, supportive environment where individuals feel heard, understood, and empowered to make meaningful changes in their lives. I value authenticity, empathy, and collaboration in the therapeutic process and strive to meet each client with respect and compassion.

I would welcome the opportunity to contribute my skills, dedication, and enthusiasm to your team. Thank you for your time and consideration. I look forward to discussing how my education, clinical training, and passion for helping others align with the needs of your organization.

Sincerely,

Aubrey Pearson
